Mission Statement

A forum for open-system methods.

OQSFP exists to connect researchers using open quantum system ideas in fundamental physics. The goal is to make methods, questions, and results visible across communities that often work on similar non-equilibrium problems with different languages and tools.

Interested in participating?

Researchers working on open quantum systems, field theory, thermal physics, quantum simulation, or related topics are welcome to get in touch.
